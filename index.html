Sichuan Spicy Hot Pot 四川火锅
壹 02 03

The Pot is a design project by Eunjung Paik, Ran Tao, Rossa Nagang Kim, and Wendy Wei, a group of graduate students from Carnegie Mellon University.

America has been coined "the melting pot." People from all across the world come to make America their home, bringing and sharing the flavors and foods of their native cultures. These food come with stories to tell. We all come from different countries in East Asia and therefore want to tell great stories behind our soul food.
